If you or someone you love have been diagnosed with Large Granular Lymphocytic (LGL) Leukemia, please join our Support Group.

The purpose of this CLOSED private support group and community, is to promote a broad patient/caregiver understanding and knowledge of LGL Leukemia and support for LGLL patients/families and caregivers only. Only group MEMBERS can view and post here. Absolutely NO solicitation or trolling will be tolerated and you will be removed and banned from the group.

Feel free to introduce yourself and share your LGLL journey and ask questions, whenever you are ready. We are a non-judgemental, caring, friendly, open-minded group of people with similar challenges and questions, while navigating the unknown waters of Large Granular Lymphocytic Leukemia.

Please be kind, empathetic and sensitive to others when posting. We are ALL learning and all questions are good ones. Please feel free to get to know one another and it is strongly encouraged to laugh, as laughter is the best medicine.


DISCLAIMER: THIS CLOSED SUPPORT GROUP DOES NOT PROVIDE MEDICAL ADVICE. This closed group is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health care provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or treatment and before undertaking a new health care regimen, and never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this closed support group. The information, including but not limited to, text, graphics, images, links and other material contained on this closed support group are for LGL Leukemia informational and support purposes only.
