MISSION STATEMENT

The Large Granular Lymphocytic Leukemia Foundation is focused on creating awareness, resources, and support for those who are newly diagnosed or are existing patients with LGLL and/or caregivers of those with this very rare form of leukemia. We are here for you and know You are not alone. “The Face of Cancer Can Be Very Deceiving.” You never know who may have it; your neighbor, your co-worker, etc..

Be KIND. Show EMPATHY. Give LOVE. ♥
